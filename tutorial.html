THE BASICS

WHAT IS CUTTER?

A Discord bot that tracks every operation — who farmed, funded, cooked, and sold — and splits the haul fair, automatically.

  • Log the whole op in its own channel — deposits, cooks, and sales
  • Settle pays out by capital, work, rank, and selling risk — to the dollar
  • Every payout is on the record in a read-only archive — no disputes

THE SPLIT

FOUR WAYS TO GET PAID

①Capital back

Front materials or cash — reimbursed in full before any profit.

②Work · 70%

Farm, fund, or cook. Paid by what you put in.

③Rank · 30%

Your level in the chain, read from your Discord role.

④Commission

Hazard pay for moving product — you hold the heat.

Big take-home numbers are mostly your own capital coming back — the payout shows capital vs. earned separately.

WHEN THINGS GO SIDEWAYS

FIXES & HELP

  • Wrong number? Officers can /void any entry — it's logged, never deleted
  • Already settled but need a fix? Officers /job reopen → correct → /settle again
  • Check /status before settling — it flags product made but not sold
  • Settled channels are read-only archives with the full record — that's your receipt

Stuck? Ping an officer.
